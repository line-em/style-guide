About / Changelog

A style guide is a useful tool to keep a similar look and feel throughout different pages, media, etc.

Therefore, this project is made to keep a consistent design throughout my projects and pages. However, during my studies, it may be possible to tweak these rules to better fit my identity, to polish it, etc.

When that happens, I will update changelog here to log all such changes.
